Course Rationale:

to Biology & Chemistry for Computation has been the most used methods of incorporating intelligence of chemistry & the biological world to computer science.
It is, therefore, necessary to develop a good understanding of their operation and how they can be used as building blocks for the computerized application of biology and chemistry.
This course explores the inner workings of chemistry and biological world from the programmer,s perspective by implementing different algorithms of Computer Science.

Course Objectives:

To provide a solid conceptual understanding of biology and chemistry for computation. More specifically,

  • To gather knowledge about chemistry & biological world and relation with Computer Science and Engineering

  • To analyze some existing methods and algorithms for specific chemistry & biological problems

  • To grow the research interest in the field of Bioinformatics, Biochemistry and the Chemical industry

  • To know the scope of the bioinformatics, bio chemistry and chemical industry research area and motivate them for research work.


Course Learning Outcomes (CLO's):

CLO1 Understanding the implications, impacts and benefits of Chemistry and Biology for Computation.
CLO2 Learn the basics of Molecular and Cellular Biology.
CLO3 Differentiate among different Computational Chemistry methods.
CLO4 Implement Different Algorithms related to Computational Biology.
CLO5 Be familiar with the importance of computation in Chemical Industries.
